Destinations

Travel has a way of expanding how you see problems — and people. Here are three places on my list.

  1. Bali, Indonesia
    • Famous for terraced rice fields and temple culture
    • World-class surfing at Uluwatu and Canggu
    • Vibrant food scene and creative expat community
  2. Ibiza, Spain
    • Crystal-clear Mediterranean beaches
    • UNESCO-listed old town (Dalt Vila)
    • Gateway to exploring the rest of the Balearic Islands
  3. Guatemala
    • Stunning Lake Atitlán surrounded by volcanoes
    • Rich Mayan heritage and colonial architecture in Antigua
    • One of the most biodiverse countries in Central America
